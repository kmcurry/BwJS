Bridgeborn

Founded in 2002, Bridgeborn is a privately held company headquartered in Virginia Beach, VA with offices in Arlington, VA, and Southern Pines, NC. Bridgeborn is a leading Information Technology, Operations, Policy and Research company that serves the Civilian, Defense, Homeland Security and Intelligence communities. Bridgeborn enables visibility and transparency through advanced visualization and reporting using rapid and agile methodologies to deliver low risk, high value solutions. We provide mission focused support services to a range of customers including USAID, DHS/FEMA, DARPA, US Army, JIEDDO, US Air Force and the Department of Justice.
